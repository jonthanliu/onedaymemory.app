Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 04, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to OneDay Memory ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles your data and respects your privacy rights.

2. Data Collection and Usage

OneDay Memory is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, or share your personal data on our servers.

2.1 Photo Library Access

  • Purpose: Our app requires access to your Photo Library to display photos, organize them into "Memories" and "Galleries," and allow you to add notes to them.
  • Processing: All photo processing and organization happen locally on your device.
  • Storage: We do not upload your photos to any external server owned by us.

2.2 Location Data

  • Purpose: We may request access to your location data to display the location where a photo was taken and to fetch historical weather data for your memories.
  • Storage: Location metadata is stored locally within the app's database on your device.

2.3 User-Generated Content

Any text, notes, or "memories" you create within the app are stored locally on your device.

3. Data Storage and Sync (iCloud)

  • iCloud Sync: OneDay Memory uses Apple's CloudKit (iCloud) to synchronize your data (memories, gallery organization, and notes) across your own Apple devices.
  • Security: Your data is stored in your private iCloud container. We (the developers) do not have access to your iCloud data.
  • Control: You can manage or delete your iCloud data at any time through your device's Apple ID settings.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not use third-party analytics SDKs or advertising networks that track your behavior.

  • Weather Data: The app may use Apple's WeatherKit or similar system services to retrieve weather information. This data is processed according to Apple's privacy standards.

5. Data Retention

Since we do not store your data on our servers, you retain full control over your data retention. Deleting the app or removing data from within the app will remove it from your local device. If you have enabled iCloud backup, data may remain in your iCloud account until you delete it.
